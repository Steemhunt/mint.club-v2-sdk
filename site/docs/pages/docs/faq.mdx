# FAQ

::::note
:::details[Which chains does Mint Club support?]
The SDK supports 19 mainnets: `Ethereum`, `Base`, `Blast`, `Optimism`, `Arbitrum`, `Polygon`, `BNB Chain`, `Avalanche`, `Kaia`, `Cyber`, `Degen`, `Ham`, `Shibarium`, `Unichain`, `Zora`, `ApeChain`, `HashKey`, `Robinhood`, and `Over`.

It also supports `Sepolia`, `Base Sepolia`, `Blast Sepolia`, `Avalanche Fuji`, `Cyber Testnet`, and `Puppynet`. Optional helpers such as staking, Zap, and individual price providers are not available on every network.
:::
::::

::::note
:::details[What are the benefits of using a Bonding Curve?]
Using a Bonding Curve removes the necessity for a manual liquidity pool for tokens. The minting/burning mechanism inherent in the bonding curve contract ensures tokens or NFTs are tradable immediately upon creation. Moreover, you have the ability to tailor your tokenomics by designing the price-supply curve. This facilitates independent operation of your token/NFT, free from reliance on exchanges, centralized control, or order books.
:::
::::

::::note
:::details[Are there any disadvantages to launching ERC-20 or ERC-1155 tokens on Mint.Club?]
Mint Club assets implement standard `ERC-20` or `ERC-1155` interfaces, so other compatible applications can integrate them. Exchange listings and third-party liquidity remain separate decisions made by those platforms and liquidity providers.
:::
::::

::::note
:::details[Can I edit the curve after launching the token?]
No. The bonding curve steps are stored onchain when the asset is created and cannot be changed afterward.
:::
::::

::::note
:::details[What are the differences between Mint Club V1 and V2?]

- `Mint Club V1`: Supports BEP-20 tokens on BNB Chain with MINT as the reserve asset.

- `Mint Club V2`: Supports ERC-20 and ERC-1155 assets across multiple EVM networks, uses an ERC-20 reserve asset selected at creation, and supports customizable step-based curves.
  :::
  ::::

::::note
:::details[Can I monetize as a creator of the bonding curve token/NFT?]
Yes. A creator can configure mint and burn royalties from 0% to 50% when creating an asset. Accrued creator royalties can be claimed through the Bond contract.
:::
::::

::::note
:::details[Can I do airdrop / private sales with the token I created?]
Yes. A curve can include a zero-price creator allocation, and the protocol also provides Merkle airdrop and token lockup contracts.
:::
::::
